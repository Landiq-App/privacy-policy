LandIQ Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 18, 2024

Introduction

LandIQ ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our real estate analysis mobile application.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide:

  • Property addresses and location data for analysis
  • Land area and property specifications
  • Email address (if you create an account)
  • Payment information (processed securely via Google Play)

Automatically Collected Information:

  • Device information (model, operating system)
  • App usage analytics (features used, session duration)
  • Crash reports and performance data

How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide AI-powered real estate analysis
  • Process your analysis requests
  • Improve app functionality and user experience
  • Send important app updates and notifications
  • Process subscriptions and payments
  • Provide customer support

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Claude AI (Anthropic): For property analysis processing
  • Google Play Services: For app distribution and payments
  • Firebase: For analytics and crash reporting

These services have their own privacy policies governing the use of your information.

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information:

  • Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS)
  • Secure API authentication
  • No storage of sensitive payment information
  • Regular security updates

Data Retention

We retain your data only as long as necessary to provide our services:

  • Analysis history: 12 months
  • Account information: Until account deletion
  • Analytics data: 24 months

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request data correction or deletion
  • Opt-out of marketing communications
  • Export your analysis history
  • Delete your account

Location Data

LandIQ uses location data only when you explicitly provide it for property analysis. We do not track your location continuously or store location history without your consent.

Children's Privacy

LandIQ is not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of significant changes through the app or via email.
